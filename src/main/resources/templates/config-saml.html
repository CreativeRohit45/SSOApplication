Configure SAML 2.0 Service Provider

Controls whether the 'Login with SSO (SAML)' button appears on the login page.

Configure settings for connecting to a SAML Identity Provider (IdP).

Identity Provider (IdP) Details

Used for signature validation. Loaded from DB on each login.

Your Application (Service Provider - SP) Details

Unique identifier for your application. This must be registered with the IdP.

Your Assertion Consumer Service (ACS) URL to register with the IdP is typically:
http://localhost:8080/login/saml2/sso/{registrationId}

Attribute Mapping (Optional)

The name of the SAML attribute sent by the IdP containing the user's email.

The name of the SAML attribute sent by the IdP containing the user's display name.
